Joie is on a mission to help you build a healthier and happier life – one step at a time.

Joie pairs you with world-class teachers, experts and coaches to help you build a healthier and happier life – one step at a time. Our online platform helps you find the right programs to help you reach your goals faster and covers a variety of topics related to holistic health and wellness.

Joie's philosophy embracing a holistic wellness approach, through our 5 pillars: Movement, Emotions, Connections, Spirit, and Professional, which plays a pivotal role in your overall well-being and life-balance.

We believe that happiness is a skill that has to be taught. We are trying to redeﬁne how people look at happiness. That it is not just a feeling that comes and goes, but through learning and taking small steps we can make changes with ourselves. It does not matter where we start as long as we can take the next step to where we are going.

We welcome you to a community of people who are working for a common goal.

Who we are?

Jason Teitelbaum

As a special education teacher for children, he found satisfaction in helping children accomplished what often others said they could not.
His solution - teaching essential life skills as a way to overcome obstacles and be truly happy.
He then decided to found Joie, rest on the belief that grown-ups should learn too.
His vision is to create a community of people who share the same interest - work on being the best version of yourself. Together, the journey will be easier.

jason@joie.co
